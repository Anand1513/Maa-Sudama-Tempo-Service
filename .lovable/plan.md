

# MSTS — Public Website (Phase 1)

## Overview
Build the customer-facing public website for Maa Sudama Tempo Service with a cinematic dark navy (#0B0F1A) + gold (#F5A623) + white premium logistics aesthetic. All pages responsive, no login required.

- **Stats Row**: Animated counters — 8+ Years | 2,400+ Trips | 60+ Companies | 4 Vehicle Types | 100% On-time
